in order to grow, there needs to be change.

P1 – INTRODUCTION

Yet, embracing change is easier said than done. It can be as daunting as standing on the edge of a cliff, ready to take a leap of faith into the unknown. But that's exactly what it takes to achieve success.

      Sometimes, we must take a few steps back to gain the momentum we need to move forward. Other times, we must have the courage to face ourselves and answer the toughest questions with honesty and vulnerability.
         This is true for individuals, teams, and entire businesses. For some, change is a frightening prospect, while for others, it is the breath of life that propels them forward. Curious to know which camp we belong to?

Refokus was born during the pandemic, and because of that, change has been a cornerstone of our mission, ways of operating and even brand name from the start. We want to shake the agency world up, think differently about our approach, and create a fully-remote culture that connects people from all walks of life who share passion for what they do.

        In the first 18 months, we skyrocketed, seeing much success through our brand positioning and a longstanding partnership with Webflow, putting a little elbow grease into our marketing and delivering without compromises for over 40 clients.
       That early growth was fuel to our fire and we established ourselves as a leading agency in the Webflow and no-code space. But, we knew something even bigger was coming out of the other end of the first social media era pandemic of our time.

P2 – THE LANDSCAPE

It’s February 2023 and there are a lot of questions around the state of the world. You read news cycles, and it's all doom and gloom, with very little good news populating our feeds.

With this grim recession times looming, we need to think about the way we work differently – how we spend our time, how we leverage resources and how we measure and track the success of our work.

We need to be more entrepreneurial, working with greater urgency, sharper focus and more hunger than we’ve shown on sunnier days.

Even the CEO of Google,
Sundar Pichai, said it himself.
In other words: we need to refokus.

No longer are the days of acceptable bullshit. We need to tighten up, work smart and hard, while concentrating on the work effort that actually makes an impact. And that’s exactly what our core beliefs align with when we started this agency in January 2021.

adding up to the market tumult, a highly disruptive technology is getting mainstream and it’s shifting paradigms on how we work.

P3 – EMERGING TECH
As a no-code agency, we use tech to make our production processes faster and smoother, so our human talent can focus on what really matters for our clients and partners: maximum value and growth.
The use of AI shares these same principles, and we are already embedding the technology into different aspects of our ways of working – mostly to augment creative processes and to optimize web experiences that uplift conversion metrics.

P4 – THE BIG APPLE
So why New York, you might be wondering? Well, we love bagels so much that we wanted to be closer to the motherland –where you can find the most amazing doughy delights on every corner. But besides that, we believe with headquarters in New York – and supporting satellite teams around the world in Europe, South America and Australia – we can help even more companies think about how to streamline their efforts.
But aside from that, we recognize the challenges that our industry is facing overall and we want to help change that. Being in the city that never sleeps allows us to share our perspectives, best practices and learnings in a way that can reach many, as helping our industry reach its next iteration is a long-term goal of ours.
We want to not only help those we partner with to focus on the things that really matter, such as strategic design and growth optimization, but cut down on unnecessary costs, while still producing high-quality, game changing, industry-leading brands and websites. And there’s no better place to set up shop than one of the biggest meccas for creativity and innovation in the world – New York.

P5 – GLOBAL REACH

As a remote team, the ability to tap into talent from all corners of the world has proven to be a wildly successful model. Our ability to handpick the perfect individuals for any given project – based on their unique expertise, interests, past experiences, and cultural nuances – has given us a competitive edge.

        Not only do we create synergistic teams that work like a well-oiled machine in their respective niches, but we also streamline our processes by building repeatable teams (called hive minds) that cut down on production time.
      From Europe to South America and Australia, we've assembled an array of world-class talent. Now, we're thrilled to welcome the brightest minds from the United States to join the hive.

as we kick off 2023 with our sights set on growth and progress, we are also mindful of the challenges ahead.

P6 – REFOKUS WITH US
We recognize that the global economy is unpredictable, and we've learned from past failures to prepare for what's to come. Yet, with cautious optimism, we are eager to make a mark in the USA market, bringing our global expertise, no-code solutions and AI perspective to the table. As we step into this new territory, we are not just focused on making a splash.
We know that true growth comes from listening and learning from others. It's about forging meaningful connections and partnerships that will propel everyone forward. We understand that this approach is the key to navigating uncertain times and overcoming obstacles with grace. And, we invite all who share our vision to join us on this journey of shifting focus, growth and transformation.
